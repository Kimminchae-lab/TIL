### AAC의 다섯가지 라이브러리
> **LifeCycles** (Easy handling lifecycles)
> **LiveData** (Lifecycle aware observable)
> **ViewModel** (Managing data in a lifecycle)
> **Room** (Object Mapping for SQLite)
> **Paging** (Gradually loading information)
